About Me

Hi there. I am Md. Mamun Sheikh. I completed my undergraduate degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh. My research interests include Applied Machine Learning and Data Science. I have previously published several papers on applying ML and AI techniques in healthcare and data science.

I achieved several international awards, including the 1st Place Award with 0.1M JPY prize money in the Heatstroke Prevention Challenge under the 5th International Conference on Activity and Behavior Computation (ABC). In addition, I secured the 1st Place Award with prize money in the 3rd Nurse Care Activity Recognition Challenge, which is part of the HASCA workshop at ACM UbiComp, 2021. Moreover, I accomplished the Best Paper Award (Bronze Edition) at the Joint 10th International Conference on Informatics, Electronics & Vision (ICIEV) and the 5th International Conference on Imaging, Vision & Pattern Recognition (icIVPR).
